② Contrast Enhancement L1 — Text
Darker text · Bold · Wider line spacing (short dwell)
🎨
③ Contrast Enhancement L2 — Component

🗣️
④ Voice Reading — Long hold trigger

🎨
⑤ Contrast Enhancement L3 — Full Page
Triggered on abnormal scan · Full-page contrast applied
✦
Focus Attraction — Blind-spot exclusive
Mutually exclusive with contrast enhancement · Pulse dot at visual boundary
🗺️ Cold Zone Detection
If a screen quadrant has no gaze hold for a long period, AI infers it as a blind-spot area and switches to focus attraction instead of contrast enhancement
